CricInsight - Cricket Teams in the Upcoming Season

Mumbai Indians Squad

The Mumbai Indians squad for the upcoming season includes captain Rohit Sharma, along with exciting talents like Dewald Brevis, Suryakumar Yadav, Ishan Kishan, and N. Tilak Verma. Tim David, Vishnu Vinod, and the promising Arjun Tendulkar add depth to the team. Notable players such as Hardik Pandya, Jasprit Bumrah, and Jason Behrendorff strengthen their lineup.

Chennai Super Kings Squad

Under the leadership of MS Dhoni, the Chennai Super Kings boast a squad featuring Moeen Ali, Deepak Chahar, and the talented Devon Conway. Ruturaj Gaikwad, Ravindra Jadeja, and the experienced Ajinkya Rahane contribute to the team's balanced composition.

Gujarat Titans Squad

Shubman Gill captains the Gujarat Titans, supported by international stars like David Miller, Matthew Wade, and Kane Williamson. The team also features promising talents like B. Sai Sudharsan, Rahul Tewatia, and the dynamic Rashid Khan.

Delhi Capitals Squad

Rishabh Pant leads the Delhi Capitals, featuring a mix of established players and rising stars. The squad includes David Warner, Prithvi Shaw, Anrich Nortje, and the experienced Ishant Sharma, creating a formidable lineup.

Lucknow Super Giants Squad

KL Rahul captains the Lucknow Super Giants, with a lineup that includes Quinton de Kock, Nicholas Pooran, and the explosive Marcus Stoinis. The team also features talented youngsters like Ayush Badoni and Yash Thakur.

Rajasthan Royals Squad

Sanju Samson captains the Rajasthan Royals, with a squad comprising Jos Buttler, Shimron Hetmyer, and the promising Yashasvi Jaiswal. The team also boasts a strong bowling attack with Trent Boult, Yuzvendra Chahal, and Adam Zampa.

Sunrisers Hyderabad Squad

The Sunrisers Hyderabad squad features exciting talents like Abdul Samad, Aiden Markram, and Rahul Tripathi. Bhuvneshwar Kumar leads the bowling department, supported by T Natarajan and the experienced Mayank Agarwal.

Royal Challengers Bangalore Squad

Faf du Plessis captains the Royal Challengers Bangalore, with a star-studded lineup including Glenn Maxwell, Virat Kohli, and the explosive Rajat Patidar. The team also features promising youngsters like Suyash Prabhudessai and Will Jacks.

Punjab Kings Squad

Shikhar Dhawan leads the Punjab Kings, featuring a squad with a mix of international and domestic talents. The team includes Liam Livingstone, Sam Curran, Kagiso Rabada, and the promising Rahul Chahar.

Kolkata Knight Riders Squad

The Kolkata Knight Riders boast a squad with dynamic players such as Nitish Rana, Shreyas Iyer, and Sunil Narine. The team also features exciting talents like Rahmanullah Gurbaz and Jason Roy.
